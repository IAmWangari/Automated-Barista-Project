Coffee Recipe

More Coffee

More Coffee ives you a really good frothy cofee.

Ingredients

1.5 teaspoons instant coffee
1.5 teaspoons sugar or add as per taste
3 tablespoons hot water
1 cup milk

Instructions

In a cup adds it adds the instant coffee and sugar.
Then adds the three tablespoons of hot boiling water.
Beats the coffee till its color lightens and you see a frothy layer on top.
It then takes 1 cup of milk and boils it on a medium - high heat.
When the milk comes to boil, it pours the milk in a glass jar.
Now it shakes the jar vigourously for 1 to 2 minutes.
There should be a nice layer of frothy foam on the milk.
It proceeds to pour the foamy milk in the cup containing coffee.
After adding 1/2 of the milk to the coffee it sirs with a spoon.
Then pours remaining milk together with the frothy layer.
Sprinkles ground cinnamon powder on top.
Serves coffee when hot.
